# SDC Feature Catalog

Source: Structured Data Capture IG 2025-10 v4.0.0 [doc](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/sdc/en/index.html).

This catalog captures the questionnaire-centric capabilities defined by the HL7 SDC implementation guide so renderer evaluations can reference a consistent feature baseline. The lists group artifacts roughly by the way a renderer would surface or integrate them.

## Out of Scope (Renderer)

- Behavior: Capability Statements are out of scope for the renderer (system-level conformance).
- Behavior: Search Parameters are out of scope for the renderer (repository/search behavior).
- Resource Profiles: parameters-_, sdc-questionnaire-pop-_, sdc-questionnaire-extr-\*, sdc-questionnaire-search, sdc-questionnaire-adapt-srch, sdc-servicerequest, sdc-task, sdc-library, sdc-valueset, sdc-codesystem are out of scope for the renderer.
- Data Type Profiles are out of scope for the renderer (data type constraints).
- Operation Definitions are out of scope for the renderer (server operations).
- Structures: Logical Models are out of scope for the renderer (reference-only artifacts).
- Structures: Questionnaires are out of scope for the renderer (example/definition artifacts).
- Structures: Extension Definitions are out of scope for the renderer (spec definitions, not renderer behavior).
- Terminology: Value Sets are out of scope for the renderer (terminology catalogs).
- Terminology: Code Systems are out of scope for the renderer (terminology catalogs).
- Terminology: Structure Maps are out of scope for the renderer (transformation artifacts).
- Example: Example Instances are out of scope for the renderer (illustrative data only).
